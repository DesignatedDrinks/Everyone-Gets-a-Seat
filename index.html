The book

This is not a polished redemption story.

A man stops drinking. That is where the easy version of the story ends—and where this book begins.

Everyone Gets a Seat follows what happened after Mike’s family left, after the final beer, and after the promises had to become visible behaviour. Kristen tells the story from the seat that recovery stories too often push to the edge: the person who lived beside the drinking, protected the children, left, and chose for herself whether to return.

Together, Mike and Kristen examine marriage, family, ambition, and the company that grew from their belief that belonging should not depend on what is in your glass.

The authors

Mike & Kristen Norris

Educators. Entrepreneurs. Partners telling both sides of one complicated story.

Kristen Norris is an educator and entrepreneur who has spent 22 years teaching and holds a Master of Education. Her work has centred on helping people grow, building environments where they feel seen, and proving that compassion and clear boundaries can exist together.

Mike Norris is an educator, entrepreneur, and tool and die maker by trade. He holds a degree in education, and his career has crossed manufacturing, skilled-trades education, college instruction, educational technology, and secondary-school teaching.

Together, Mike and Kristen have turned their lived experience into both a business and a broader conversation about belonging. Through Designated Drinks, their writing, and their speaking, they explore life after alcohol, rebuilding trust, parenting through uncertainty, and creating families and organizations where no one has to disappear to belong.
